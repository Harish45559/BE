How to place your order
1
Scan the QR code
Open your phone camera and point it at the QR code above. Tap the link that appears.
2
Create an account or log in
First time? Tap Register and enter your name, email & password. Takes less than a minute.
3
Browse the menu
Scroll through categories, tap any dish to add it to your cart. Use the Veg / Non-Veg filter or search by name.
☕ Breakfast hours (9am–11:45am): only breakfast items are shown. Full menu available from 5:15pm.
4
Go to your cart
Tap the cart icon at the top. Review your items and quantities, then tap Checkout.
5
Choose Takeaway or Eat In
At checkout, select 🥡 Takeaway to collect and take your food away, or 🍽️ Eat In to dine with us at the restaurant. All menu items are available for both options.
6
Choose pickup time & pay
Select your preferred pickup time. Pay online by card.
7
Wait for your order
You'll see your order status update live. Come in at your pickup time and collect your food!
